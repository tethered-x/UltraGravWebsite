022: Well that's a wrap on that

Game development is difficult, and I no longer feel like that is a viable thing for me to do with the little time I have for creative projects. I just want to tell my stories and I am wasting a bunch of time figuring out game dev. Also, too much time in front of my computer and not enough time around my family. I'm not going to pursue game development at this time. Instead, I am just going to focus on one creative outlet and that is making comics. I already have plans to finish "Vroom Verse" by the end of the year. Then next year, instead of making a game, I'll be turning "Ship Runners" into a graphic novel. Some of which will be an adaptation of a short story my daughter wrote for her favorite character, The Thing.
